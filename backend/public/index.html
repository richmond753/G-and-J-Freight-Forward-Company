About the Company

Your Cargo,
Our Commitment

G and J Freight Services Limited is a Freight Forwarder and a full Non-Vessel Operating Cargo Carrier (NVOCC) company which has been in business since November 8, 2008.

We have quickly become a major player in clearing and forwarding, air freight, movers and packaging, transport and haulage, consolidation and deconsolidation services — offering a comprehensive range of logistics solutions tailored to every client's need.

We have developed and obtained the trust of almost all major shipping carriers and airlines, gaining very competitive service contract rates which we pass directly to our valued customers.

Our office is located at Ground Floor, Kpodo Plaza, Opposite Creator School, Tema Comm 4, Accra – Ghana.
